FAQ

Common Questions,
Clear Answers

Everything you need to know before beginning your UPSC journey with us.

Have a question that's not covered here? Our counsellors are available to guide you through your enrolment decision.

📞 Speak to a Counsellor
When should I start my UPSC preparation?
▾
Ideally, start at least 12-18 months before your first Prelims attempt. However, a focused 9-month crash preparation with our Intensive Prelims program has also helped students clear the exam. The right time is now — consistent preparation always pays.
Do you offer online classes for students outside Delhi?
▾
Yes. Our Live Online Batch provides the same quality of instruction as our classroom program with HD live streaming, recorded lectures for revision, online test platform access, and doubt-clearing sessions — fully accessible from any location in India.
What optional subjects are available?
▾
We currently offer dedicated optional subject programs in Political Science & IR (PSIR), Sociology, History, Geography, and Public Administration. Each optional is taught by a specialist faculty member with a track record of producing toppers in that subject.
Is there a trial class or free demo available?
▾
Yes, we offer a free one-week trial for our online batch and free demo classes at our centre every Saturday. You can also access our free daily current affairs, weekly MCQ quiz, and sample study material without any registration.
What is the fee structure and are EMIs available?
▾
Fee varies by program — our GS Foundation starts at ₹85,000 and online batches at ₹45,000. We offer 0% interest EMI options and merit-based fee waivers for economically weaker sections. Contact our counselling team for a personalised fee plan.
How is copy evaluation handled for Mains?
▾
Each Mains answer copy is reviewed by two evaluators — one subject specialist and one retired UPSC board member. You receive a scored evaluation with marginal comments, model answers, and a one-on-one session with your mentor within 7 working days.
